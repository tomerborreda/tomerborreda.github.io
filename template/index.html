Abstract

We introduce TBD, a novel framework for learning a controllable generative model of 4D scenes (3D space + time). Unlike previous methods that generate entire sequences at once, our approach models the underlying dynamics of a scene, allowing for long-term, consistent video generation and interactive control.

By decomposing motion and appearance, our model can be prompted with an initial state and then generate plausible future states frame-by-frame. We demonstrate state-of-the-art results on complex datasets of moving objects and dynamic natural scenes, showing significant improvements in temporal coherence and user controllability.
